Everything You Need
Built for leaders who
actually give a damn.

Every feature is designed around one goal: making sure no team member ever feels forgotten or unsupported.

📧

Email Follow-Up Tracker

Monitors your inbox and flags unanswered emails from team members. Get a one-click draft reply so you never leave someone hanging for days.

📅

Review Cycle Engine

Tracks every 30, 60, and 90-day milestone, annual reviews, and performance check-ins. Reminded days in advance so you're always prepared.

💬

Teams, Slack & Email Alerts

Nudges delivered where you already work. Connect Microsoft Teams, Gmail, Outlook, or Slack — alerts come to you, not the other way around.

📋

AI-Powered 1:1 Agendas

Auto-generates weekly 1:1 agendas based on open action items, project status, and team goals — sent to both you and your direct report before every meeting.

📊

Pulse Check Surveys

Send anonymous weekly pulse surveys to your team in one click. Track morale trends over time and spot disengagement early before it becomes turnover.

🧠

AI Manager Coaching

Get real-time AI suggestions on handling difficult conversations, giving constructive feedback, and supporting a struggling team member with empathy.

🏢

Company Admin Dashboard

Directors and VPs get a rollup view across all managers and teams — see engagement scores, review completion rates, and flag risk areas at a glance.

🔗

HRIS Integrations

Sync directly with BambooHR, Workday, and Rippling so start dates, titles, and org structure stay automatically up to date — zero manual entry.

📈

Manager Scorecards

Monthly scorecard showing your follow-up rate, 1:1 completion, and team sentiment trend. Know exactly where to improve as a leader.
