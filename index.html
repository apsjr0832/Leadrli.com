Whether you're leading a team or just trying to have a great workday — Leadrli has the tools to help you stay organized, follow through, and feel good about your work.

📧

Follow-Up Tracker

Never leave an email on read too long. Leadrli watches your inbox and nudges you when someone's waiting — whether it's your boss, a client, or a direct report.

📅

Review & Milestone Reminders

Managers get 30/60/90-day review alerts. Employees get deadline and project reminders. Nobody misses an important moment again.

💬

Teams, Slack & Email Alerts

Reminders land where you already work — Microsoft Teams, Slack, Gmail, or Outlook. No new app to check, no friction, just the right nudge at the right time.

🤖

AI Email Drafting

Stuck on how to word that follow-up or feedback email? Type a few words, pick your tone, and Leadrli writes a full professional email in seconds. For everyone.

🎯

Focus Mode & Daily Briefing

Start every morning knowing exactly what matters. Set your #1 goal, block distractions with a built-in Pomodoro timer, and end the day having actually done the work.

👏

Kudos & Recognition Board

Anyone on the team can celebrate anyone else. Peer-to-peer recognition builds culture, boosts morale, and makes people feel seen — not just managers giving praise top-down.

🏆

Weekly Wins Log

Employees track their own accomplishments week by week. Managers see team momentum. HR uses it for performance reviews. Everyone builds a record they're proud of.

💚

Mood & Wellbeing Check-In

A quick daily check-in that helps employees reflect on how they're feeling. Managers see team-wide trends. HR spots burnout risk before it becomes a real problem.

🏢

Company & HR Dashboard

HR leaders and executives get a live view of engagement, manager performance, onboarding health, and retention risk — across the entire organization in one screen.
